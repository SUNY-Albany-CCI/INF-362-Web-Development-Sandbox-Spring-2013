• Punctuality


    In order to maintain a job, time is of the essence. Multiple other people rely on your timing and ability to show up to work. The more time efficient you are, the more you can get done while at work.

  • Dedication


    There will be times in which you won't to work or feel like your work isn't being acknowledged. Dedication will be the glue to keep you motivated and focused on the greater good of the outcome.

  • Determination


    Be determined that you are certain you will be successful.

  • Optimism


    Even when you have doubts think of the greater good of the outcome. There will be times when you feel like you are investing time into something that will be a fail. Make yourself find positive viewpoints about certain duties you will be assigned.

  • Pre-planning


    The more organized you are the more efficient you will become.

  • Communication


    As I stated earlier, in a company you are one piece of the puzzle. The only way all of the pieces can come together is if everyone interacts and it starts with you.

  • Effective Teamwork


    There is no "I" in team. Everyone must play their role in order for the whole team (the company) to be successful and efficient.
