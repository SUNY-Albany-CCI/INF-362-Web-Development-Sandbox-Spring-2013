Preferred teaching practices: Use of technology in aiding teaching, allowing question's to be asked during class & hands-on activities.

Discouraged teaching practices: Strictly lecturing off powerpoint slides, not showing enough examples and teaching more in theory then in practical use. Also not allowing enough hands-on activities for student to accurately grasp concept.

Preferred learning practices: Engaging in practice of concepts taught, note taking, and using technology such as Blackboard.

Discouraged learning practices: Strictly reading assignments, having to do busy work, etc.
