Fun Facts about the German Shepherd:

  • The breed originated in Germany and was bred as a sheep dog, hence its name.
  • German Shepherds are considered to be the third most intelligent dog breed behind the border collie and the poodle.
  • President John F. Kennedy had a pet German Shepherd Dog named Clipper.
  • They are very athletic dogs and are often entered into agility and sports competitions.
  • The breed is fairly new dog breed. It began in Germany in 1899 and came to the US in 1907.
  • The most famous German Shepherd Dog was Rin Tin Tin.
  • The weight of German Shepherd Dogs range from 75 - 95 pounds.
  • The height of the German Shepherd ranges from 24 - 26 inches.
  • Life of German Shepherd Dogs: the life expectancy of the German Shepherd is between 12 - 14 years.
  • German Shepherd Dogs are among the top five most popular dogs in the United States.
