Fun Facts about Shih Tzu:

  • Shih Tzu’s originated in Tibet
  • Shih Tzu’s are alert, lively and slightly stubborn, they are very agile.
  • The name Shih Tzu mean’s “Lion”.
  • Shih Tzu’s need coats in cold weather.
  • Shih Tzu’s are among the fourteen ancient breeds of dog.
