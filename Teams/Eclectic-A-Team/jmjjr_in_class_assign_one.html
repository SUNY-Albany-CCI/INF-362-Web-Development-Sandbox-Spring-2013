Johnny's In Class Assignment Week One

My preferred method of learning is very tactile. I actually took a "what type of learner are you test" in another class this past week and found out I am a kinesthetic learner. Which makes sense because I need to feel, touch, and do to learn. I am not saying I cannot learn other ways; this is just my preferred way of gathering information.

My discouraged practices in learning would have to be reading from a textbook or just listening to a professor lecture all evening long. It is very discouraging because I get bored easily and I end up wanting to fall alseep.
