Preferred practices in teaching:

  • Engage students in active learning experiences

  • Provide students with regular, reasonable and specific feedback based on their performances

  • Be able to recognize and stretch students learning styles and developmental levels

  • Always provide and present students with updated knowledge and real-world applications

  • Create activities for student-instructor interactions

  • Create activities for student-student interactions

Discouraged practices in teaching:

  • Ignore engaging students in active learning experiences

  • Provide unreasonable or irregular feedback to students

  • Unable to recognize and stretch students learning styles and developmental levels

  • Let students to work individually, without any group activities

Preferred practices in learning:

  • Do every homework assignments

  • Always ask questions

  • Concentratedly listen to lectures

  • No cheating!

  • Sharing knowledge and opinions with other students or in group activities

Discouraged practices in learning:

  • Not attend classes

  • Ignore doing homework assignments

  • Ignore speaking or sharing knowledge and opinions to other students

  • Ignore asking questions and sit silently doing nothing throughout the class time
